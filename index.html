Nuestra Historia

Home cooking in the heart of downtown.

Lindo Mexico is the family-owned corner of downtown Burlington where regulars are greeted by name and first-timers leave as regulars. You'll find us at 622 Jefferson Street, in one of downtown's historic storefronts — the kind of place where the owner still comes out to say hello.

Everything is made the way it always has been: fresh chips and homemade salsa the moment you sit down, generous plates of enchiladas, sizzling fajitas and chimichangas, and a warm, unhurried welcome. We keep it simple and honest — cash or check only, just like day one.
